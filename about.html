About me

Hey! I'm Erika.

UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design and a strong foundation in visual design. I have experience working in companies, Startups and as a Freelancer. I help transform ideas and concepts into enjoyable and appealing designs. My work is very diverse, including experience in Branding, Marketing, Web and Screen design. I’ve always had a user-centered mentality, focusing on understanding what my customers want and need so as to deliver high-quality desirable products.

After some time working closely with product and developing teams, improving and testing products and designs, I felt more and more attracted to this field and it felt natural to move into user experience design.

Incorporating both my graphic design expertise and creativity, my goal is to make a difference in an innovative team, to meaningfully improve people’s lives by creating simple, delightful and satisfying product experiences and solutions.
